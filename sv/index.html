Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right dog breed for my lifestyle?

Consider your living space, activity level, time for grooming, and family situation. Our Breed Quiz asks these questions and matches you with compatible breeds.

What are the best dog breeds for apartments?

Fransk Bulldogg, Cavalier King Charles Spaniels, Mops, and Bostonterrier adapt well to apartment living. Size isn't everything—some large breeds like Greyhounds are calm indoors.

Which breeds are best for families with children?

Labrador Retrievers, Golden Retrievers, Beagles, and Collies are known for being patient and gentle with kids. Always supervise interactions.

What dogs shed the least?

Pudel, Bichon Frisé, Portugisisk Vattenhund, and Schnauzers are low-shedding breeds. "Hypoallergenic" doesn't mean allergy-free.

How much exercise do different breeds need?

High-energy breeds need 2+ hours daily. Moderate breeds need about 1 hour. Low-energy breeds are happy with 30 minutes.

What's the difference between the Compare tool and the Quiz?

The Quiz helps you discover breeds based on your lifestyle. The Compare tool lets you view two specific breeds side-by-side.
