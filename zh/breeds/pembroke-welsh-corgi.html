概述

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a strong, athletic little dog. They're one of the world's most popular herding breeds and have been favored by British royalty for decades.

性格

Corgis are smart, affectionate, and surprisingly athletic. They have big-dog personalities in compact bodies. Can be vocal and may try to herd children.

健康

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 12-15 年. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

运动

High energy breed requiring vigorous daily exercise—at least an hour of activity. They thrive with active families who enjoy outdoor activities. Mental stimulation through training and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
