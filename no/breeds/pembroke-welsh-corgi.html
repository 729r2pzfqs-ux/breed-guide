Oversikt

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi er en strong, athletic little hund. They're one of world's most popular herding rases and have been favored by British royalty for decades.

Temperament

Corger enre smart, kjærlig, and surprisingly athletic. They have big-hund personalities in compact bodies. Can be vocal and may try to herd children.

Helse

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 12-15 år. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Mosjon

High energy rase requiring vigorous daily exercise—at least an hour of activity. They thrive with aktive familier who enjoy outdoor activities. Mental stimulation through training and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
