Обзор

Not to be confused with the Cavalier, the English Toy Spaniel (King Charles Spaniel in the UK) is smaller with a domed head. They're gentle, devoted lapdogs.

Темперамент

The English Toy Spaniel is known for being gentle, happy, playful. They're excellent with children and make wonderful family pets. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded companions.

Здоровье

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 10-12 лет. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Физическая Активность

Low to moderate exercise needs with short daily walks and indoor play. They adapt well to apartment living when their basic needs are met. Mental stimulation through interactive toys keeps them engaged. Gentle activities suit this breed's calm temperament.
