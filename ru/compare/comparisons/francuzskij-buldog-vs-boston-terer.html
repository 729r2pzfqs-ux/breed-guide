🧠 Бостон Терьер Темперамент

Дружелюбный Bright Amusing Нежный Lively Умный

Boston Terriers are lively, highly intelligent, and have a gentle disposition. They're known for their tuxedo-like markings and friendly, amusing personality.

🏃 Потребность в Нагрузке

Французский Бульдог:

Moderate exercise needs—short walks and play sessions suffice. Avoid overexertion in hot weather due to their flat faces.

Бостон Терьер:

Moderate energy requiring daily walks and play. More athletic than Frenchies but still prone to overheating.

✂️ Требования по Уходу

Французский Бульдог:

Minimal grooming—weekly brushing. Clean facial wrinkles regularly to prevent infections.

Бостон Терьер:

Low maintenance—occasional brushing. Their short coat is easy to care for.

🎯 Какая Подходит Вам?

Выберите Французский Бульдог если...

  • ✓ You want a calmer, more relaxed companion
  • ✓ You prefer a stockier build
  • ✓ You live in an apartment
  • ✓ You want minimal exercise requirements
  • ✓ You love those adorable bat ears

Выберите Бостон Терьер если...

  • ✓ You want a slightly more active dog
  • ✓ You prefer the tuxedo look
  • ✓ You want a dog that's easier to train
  • ✓ You like a more athletic build
  • ✓ You want a longer-lived breed

Итог

Both are excellent apartment dogs with big personalities. Frenchies are calmer and more laid-back, while Bostons are slightly more energetic and athletic. Either will steal your heart!
