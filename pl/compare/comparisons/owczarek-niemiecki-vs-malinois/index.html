Owczarek Niemiecki:

High exercise needs—at least 2 hours daily. They excel at various dog sports, tracking, and obedience work.

Owczarek Belgijski Malinois:

Very high exercise needs—2+ hours of intense activity daily. They need mental stimulation as much as physical exercise.

✂️ Wymagania Pielęgnacji

Owczarek Niemiecki:

Moderate grooming—brush 2-3 times weekly. Heavy seasonal shedding requires more attention.

Owczarek Belgijski Malinois:

Easy grooming—weekly brushing. Shorter coat than GSD but still sheds seasonally.
