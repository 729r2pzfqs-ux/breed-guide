Przegląd

South Korea's national dog, the Jindo is fiercely loyal to their family. They're independent thinkers with strong prey drive and deep bonds with their owners.

Temperament

The Korean Jindo is known for being loyal, intelligent, bold. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded companions.

Zdrowie

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 12-15 lat. Common concerns include joint problems. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ćwiczenia

High energy breed requiring vigorous daily exercise—at least an hour of activity. They thrive with active families who enjoy outdoor activities. Mental stimulation through training and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
