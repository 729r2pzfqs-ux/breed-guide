Przegląd

Known for their distinctive wrinkles and blue-black tongue, Shar-Pei are devoted, protective dogs who bond closely with their family but can be aloof with strangers.

Temperament

The Shar-Pei is known for being loyal, independent, calm. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded companions.

Zdrowie

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 8-12 lat. Common concerns include joint problems. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ćwiczenia

Low to moderate exercise needs with short daily walks and indoor play. They adapt well to apartment living when their basic needs are met. Mental stimulation through interactive toys keeps them engaged. Gentle activities suit this breed's calm temperament.
