🧠 Tysk Schæferhund Temperament

Selvsikker Modig Klog Loyal Alsidig Beskyttende

German Shepherds are confident, courageous, and incredibly versatile. They bond deeply with family and are naturally protective without being aggressive.

🧠 Belgisk Malinois Temperament

Selvsikker Klog Hardworking Opmærksom Intense Beskyttende

Malinois are intense, driven working dogs with endless energy. They need a job to do and thrive with experienced handlers who can channel their drive.

🏃 Motionsbehov

Tysk Schæferhund:

High exercise needs—at least 2 hours daily. They excel at various dog sports, tracking, and obedience work.

Belgisk Malinois:

Very high exercise needs—2+ hours of intense activity daily. They need mental stimulation as much as physical exercise.

✂️ Plejebehov

Tysk Schæferhund:

Moderate grooming—brush 2-3 times weekly. Heavy seasonal shedding requires more attention.

Belgisk Malinois:

Easy grooming—weekly brushing. Shorter coat than GSD but still sheds seasonally.

Konklusion

Both are exceptional working dogs, but Malinois are more intense and require experienced handlers. GSDs are more versatile and better suited for families. Choose based on your experience level and lifestyle.
