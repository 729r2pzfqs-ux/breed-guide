Oversigt

The French Bulldog has enjoyed a surge in popularity. The tilpasningsdygtig Frenchie is the ideal ledsager for city dwellers—they don't need a lot of space and are content with short walks and indoor play.

Temperament

Frenchies are charmerende, tilpasningsdygtig, and make excellent ledsagers. They're legesyg but not overly energisk. They love human contact and get along well with other pets.

Sundhed

Brachycephalic breed prone to breathing issues, overheating, spinal disorders, and eye problems. Choose breeders who prioritize health over extreme features.

Motion

Low to moderate exercise needs with short daily walks and indoor play. They adapt well to apartment living when their basic needs are met. Mental stimulation through interaktiv toys keeps them engaged. Gentle activities suit this breed's rolig temperament.
