The older of the two Corgi breeds, Cardigans have a tail and rounder ears. They're versatile, kærlig dogs who were bred to herd cattle by nipping at heels.

Temperament

The Cardigan Welsh Corgi is known for being kærlig, loyal, klog. They're excellent with children and make wonderful family pets. Their intelligence and ivrigness to please make training enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded ledsagers.

Sundhed

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 12-15 år. Common concerns include joint problems. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Motion

High energy breed requiring vigorous daily exercise—at least an hour of activity. They thrive with aktiv families who enjoy outdoor activities. Mental stimulation through training and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
