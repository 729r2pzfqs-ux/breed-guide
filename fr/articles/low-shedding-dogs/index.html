20 Best Low-Shedding Dog Breeds

Love dogs but hate fur on your couch? These breeds keep shedding to a minimum—but there's a trade-off.

All dogs shed at least a little—it's how they regulate temperature. But some breeds shed so minimally you'll barely notice. The secret? Many low-shedding dogs have hair that grows continuously rather than fur that cycles.

The trade-off: Most low-shedding breeds require regular professional grooming (every 4-8 weeks). Less vacuuming, more grooming appointments!
