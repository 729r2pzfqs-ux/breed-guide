Aperçu

A versatile Spanish breed used for herding and water retrieval, the Spanish Water Dog has a distinctive curly coat and loyal personality.

Tempérament

The Spanish Water Dog is known for being diligent, affectueux, loyal. They're excellent with enfants and make wonderful family pets. Their intelligence and eagerness to please make dressage enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compagnons.

Santé

Généralement en bonne santé with a lifespan of 12-14 ans. Common concerns include problèmes articulaires. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Exercice

Haute énergie breed requiring vigorous exercice quotidien—at least an heure of activity. They thrive with active familles who enjoy outdoor activities. stimulation mentale through dressage and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
