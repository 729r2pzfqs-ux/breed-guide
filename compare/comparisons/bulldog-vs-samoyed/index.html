Detailed Analysis

When comparing the Bulldog and Samoyed, both breeds bring unique qualities to the table. The Bulldog is a medium-sized breed from England with a lifespan of 8-10 years. The Samoyed hails from Siberia, Russia and typically lives 12-14 years. Energy-wise, the Bulldog is low while the Samoyed is high, which may influence your choice depending on your activity level.
