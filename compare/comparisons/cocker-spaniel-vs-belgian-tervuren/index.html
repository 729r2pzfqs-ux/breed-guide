Detailed Analysis

When comparing the Cocker Spaniel and Belgian Tervuren, both breeds bring unique qualities to the table. The Cocker Spaniel is a medium-sized breed from United States/England with a lifespan of 10-14 years. The Belgian Tervuren hails from Belgium and typically lives 12-14 years. Energy-wise, the Cocker Spaniel is moderate while the Belgian Tervuren is very high, which may influence your choice depending on your activity level.
