Detailed Analysis

When comparing the Shih Tzu and Bichon Frise, both breeds bring unique qualities to the table. The Shih Tzu is a small-sized breed from Tibet/China with a lifespan of 10-18 years. The Bichon Frise hails from Spain/France and typically lives 14-15 years. Energy-wise, the Shih Tzu is low while the Bichon Frise is moderate, which may influence your choice depending on your activity level.
