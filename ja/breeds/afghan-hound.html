概要

The Afghan Hound is an ancient, elegant sighthound known for its stunning flowing coat and aristocratic bearing.

性格

Afghan Hounds are dignified and aloof with strangers but playful and affectionate with family. They have an independent streak that can make training challenging. At home, they often display a goofy, clownish side that surprises first-time owners.

健康

Generally healthy but prone to hip dysplasia, cataracts, and hypothyroidism. Their deep chest makes them susceptible to bloat. Regular eye exams are recommended. Life expectancy is 12-14 年 with proper care.

運動

High exercise needs requiring daily runs in a secure, fenced area. Their strong prey drive means they should never be off-leash in unfenced areas. They excel at lure coursing and agility. Mental stimulation through training helps prevent boredom.
