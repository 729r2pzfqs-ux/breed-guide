概要

チャイニーズ・クレステッド・ドッグ come in two varieties: Hairless (with hair on head, feet, and tail) and Powderpuff (fully coated).

性格

the チャイニーズ・クレステッド・ドッグ is known for being 愛情深い, 遊び好きな, 警戒心のある. Their intelligence and 熱心なness to please make training enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded コンパニオンs.

健康

Generally healthy with a lifespan of 13-18 年. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

運動

Low to moderate exercise needs with short daily walks and indoor play. They adapt well to アパート暮らし when their basic needs are met. Mental stimulation through inter活発な toys keeps them engaged. Gentle activities suit this 犬種's 穏やかな temperament.
