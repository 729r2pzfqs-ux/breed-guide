🧠 Siberische Husky Temperament

Extravert Mischievous Loyaal Vriendelijk Alert Zacht

Huskies are friendly, outgoing, and sometimes mischievous. They're pack dogs who love company and are known for their vocal nature and escape artist tendencies.

🧠 Alaskan Malamute Temperament

Aanhankelijk Loyaal Speels Dignified Toegewijd Strong

Malamutes are more dignified and less mischievous than Huskies. They're deeply loyal, affectionate with family, but can be more independent.

🏃 Bewegingsbehoefte

Siberische Husky:

Very high energy—designed to run for miles. At least 2 hours of vigorous exercise daily. They love running and pulling activities.

Alaskan Malamute:

High energy but with more endurance than speed. Long hikes and pulling work suit them well. They can overheat in warm climates.

✂️ Verzorgingseisen

Siberische Husky:

Heavy grooming—brush several times weekly. Massive seasonal shedding ("blowing coat") twice yearly.

Alaskan Malamute:

Heavy grooming similar to Husky. Their thicker coat requires regular brushing to prevent matting.

🎯 Welke Past bij Jou?

Kies een Siberische Husky als...

  • ✓ You want a medium-sized arctic breed
  • ✓ You prefer a faster, more athletic dog
  • ✓ You enjoy a talkative, vocal companion
  • ✓ You want striking blue eyes
  • ✓ You like a more playful personality

Kies een Alaskan Malamute als...

  • ✓ You want a larger, more powerful dog
  • ✓ You prefer a calmer, more dignified temperament
  • ✓ You have experience with strong breeds
  • ✓ You want a quieter dog (less howling)
  • ✓ You need a dog for pulling/carting

Conclusie

Huskies are medium-sized athletes who love to run and talk. Malamutes are powerful giants built for strength. Both shed heavily and need lots of exercise. Choose based on size preference and desired temperament.
