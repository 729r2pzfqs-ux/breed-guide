🧠 Poedel Temperament

Intelligent Actief Alert Faithful Trainable Proud

Poodles are exceptionally intelligent and eager to please. Don't let the fancy haircuts fool you—they're athletic dogs bred for hunting and retrieving.

🧠 Labrador Retriever Temperament

Vriendelijk Actief Extravert Zacht Intelligent Vertrouwend

Labs are the quintessential friendly dog—outgoing with everyone, patient with children, and eager to please. They're the ultimate family companion.

🏃 Bewegingsbehoefte

Poedel:

High energy requiring daily exercise and mental stimulation. They excel at agility, obedience, and various dog sports.

Labrador Retriever:

High energy—at least 1-2 hours daily. They love swimming, fetching, and any activity involving their humans.

✂️ Verzorgingseisen

Poedel:

High maintenance—professional grooming every 4-6 weeks. Daily brushing prevents matting. Hypoallergenic coat.

Labrador Retriever:

Easy grooming but heavy shedding. Weekly brushing, more during shedding season. Not hypoallergenic.

🎯 Welke Past bij Jou?

Kies een Poedel als...

  • ✓ You have allergies (hypoallergenic)
  • ✓ You want minimal shedding
  • ✓ You enjoy grooming or don't mind the cost
  • ✓ You want an elegant appearance
  • ✓ You want size options (toy/mini/standard)

Kies een Labrador Retriever als...

  • ✓ You prefer easy, low-cost grooming
  • ✓ You don't mind shedding
  • ✓ You want the classic family dog
  • ✓ You prefer a more casual look
  • ✓ You want a water-loving retriever

Conclusie

Both are highly intelligent and trainable. Poodles offer hypoallergenic coats but need more grooming. Labs are lower maintenance but shed heavily. Both make outstanding family companions!
