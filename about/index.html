About BreedFinder

Helping dog lovers find their perfect match

Our Story

BreedFinder started in 2026 with a simple mission: help people find the right dog for their lifestyle. We noticed that many breed guides were either too technical or too superficial — so we built something better.

Every breed page includes honest verdicts about who the breed is best for (and who should look elsewhere). No fluff, no bias toward popular breeds — just practical advice from dog lovers to dog lovers.

What We Offer

200+ Breed Profiles

Detailed information on temperament, size, exercise needs, grooming requirements, and health considerations.

Breed Matching Quiz

Answer questions about your lifestyle, living situation, and preferences to find breeds that fit you perfectly.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Compare two breeds directly to see how they stack up on size, energy, grooming, and family-friendliness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I suggest a breed to add?

Absolutely! Email us with the breed name and we'll consider adding it to our database.

Is BreedFinder free to use?

Yes, completely free. We believe everyone deserves access to quality breed information.

Where does your breed data come from?

Our information is compiled from kennel club standards (AKC, UKC, FCI), veterinary sources, and breed-specific organizations.
