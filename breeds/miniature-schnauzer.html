Overview

Miniature Schnauzers are friendly, intelligent dogs with distinctive beards. They're the most popular of the three Schnauzer sizes and make excellent family companions.

Temperament

Friendly and obedient but with terrier spirit. They're alert watchdogs with a tendency to bark. Good with children and other dogs.

Health

Generally healthy but prone to pancreatitis, diabetes, and eye problems. Keep them on a healthy diet.

Exercise

Moderate exercise needs. Daily walks and play sessions. Enjoy dog sports and training.

Is This Breed Right for You?

Best For

  • Apartment dwellers
  • Allergy sufferers
  • Families with children
  • Those wanting a small watchdog
  • Active seniors

Not Ideal For

  • Those who dislike barking
  • People wanting minimal grooming
  • Those who prefer very calm dogs

Our Verdict: Friendly, smart, and hypoallergenic. Mini Schnauzers make great apartment dogs and family companions. They need regular grooming and can be barky but are loyal and fun.
