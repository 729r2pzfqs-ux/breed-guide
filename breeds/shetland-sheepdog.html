Overview

Shelties are small herding dogs that look like miniature Rough Collies. They're intelligent, eager to please, and excel at obedience and agility.

Temperament

Shelties are bright and eager to please. They're sensitive and responsive to training. Can be reserved with strangers and vocal.

Health

Prone to eye problems, hip dysplasia, and dermatomyositis. Sensitive to certain medications.

Exercise

Moderate to high exercise needs. Excel at dog sports like agility and obedience.

Is This Breed Right for You?

Best For

  • Dog sport enthusiasts
  • Active families
  • Those wanting a trainable companion
  • Experienced owners
  • Homes with yards

Not Ideal For

  • Those who dislike barking
  • Apartment dwellers (noise)
  • People wanting a quiet dog
  • Those who dislike grooming

Our Verdict: Brilliant, eager-to-please dogs that excel at training and dog sports. Shelties are loyal family companions but can be vocal. Heavy shedders who need regular grooming.
