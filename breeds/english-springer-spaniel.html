Overview

English Springer Spaniels are friendly, athletic dogs bred to 'spring' game for hunters. They're excellent family companions and versatile sporting dogs.

Temperament

Springers are friendly, eager to please, and playful. They love people and are great with children. Active and need engagement.

Health

Generally healthy but prone to hip dysplasia, eye problems, and ear infections. Clean ears regularly.

Exercise

High exercise needs. Daily vigorous activity, swimming, and field work.

Is This Breed Right for You?

Best For

  • Active families with children
  • Hunters
  • Those wanting a friendly companion
  • Homes with yards
  • Dog sport enthusiasts

Not Ideal For

  • Apartment dwellers
  • Sedentary lifestyles
  • Those away from home often

Our Verdict: Friendly, athletic family dogs that love children and activities. Springers need regular exercise and engagement. Great for active families who want a loving, playful companion.
