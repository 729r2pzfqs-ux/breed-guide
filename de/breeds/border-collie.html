Überblick

Border Collies are considered the most intelligent dog breed. They were developed for herding sheep and have incredible work ethic and energy.

Temperament

The Border Collie is known for being smart, workaholic, energisch. Their intelligence and eagerness to please make Training enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded Begleiters.

Gesundheit

Im Allgemeinen gesund with a lifespan of 12-15 Jahre. Common concerns include Gelenkprobleme. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Bewegung

Hohe Energie breed requiring vigorous tägliche Bewegung—at least an Stunde of activity. They thrive with active Familien who enjoy outdoor activities. geistige Stimulation through Training and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
