Überblick

The Scottish Terrier is a small but confident terrier with a distinctive silhouette and unabhängig spirit.

Temperament

The Scottish Terrier is known for being confident, unabhängig, spirited. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded Begleiters.

Gesundheit

Im Allgemeinen gesund with a lifespan of 12 Jahre. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Bewegung

Moderate exercise braucht with daily Spaziergänge and regular play sessions. They enjoy a mix of physical activity and relaxation time. Interactive games and Training sessions provide geistige Stimulation. A consistent exercise routine keeps them healthy and happy.
