Überblick

German Shepherds are Arbeitshunds developed for herding sheep. Because of their strength, intelligence, trainability, and obedience, they're often preferred for many types of work including police, military, and search-and-rescue.

Temperament

GSDs are confident, courageous, and smart. They're willing to put their life on the line for loved ones. They can be aloof with strangers but are treu and liebend with family.
