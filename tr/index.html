Sıkça Sorulan Sorular

Yaşam tarzıma uygun köpek ırkını nasıl seçerim?

Consider your living space, activity level, time for grooming, and family situation. Our Irk Quizi asks these questions and matches you with compatible breeds based on your answers.

Apartmanlar için en iyi köpek ırkları hangileridir?

French İngiliz İngiliz İngiliz Bulldog, Cavalier King Charles Spaniels, Pugs, and Boston Terriers adapt well to apartment living. Size isn't everything—some large breeds like Greyhounds are surprisingly calm indoors, while small breeds like Jack Russells need lots of space to run.

Çocuklu aileler için en iyi ırklar hangileridir?

Labrador Retrievers, Golden Retrievers, Beagles, and Collies are known for being patient and gentle with kids. Always supervise interactions between dogs and young children, regardless of breed.

Hangi köpekler en az tüy döker?

Kaniş, Bichon Frises, Portekiz Su Köpeği, and Schnauzers are low-shedding breeds. Note that "hypoallergenic" doesn't mean allergy-free—all dogs produce some allergens through skin and saliva.

Farklı ırklar ne kadar egzersiz gerektirir?

High-energy breeds like Border Collies and Huskies need 2+ hours daily. Moderate breeds like Labradors need about 1 hour. Low-energy breeds like İngiliz İngiliz İngiliz Bulldog and Basset Hounds are happy with 30 minutes. Check each breed's page for specific needs.

Karşılaştırma aracı ile Quiz arasındaki fark nedir?

The Quiz helps you discover breeds based on your lifestyle. The Compare tool lets you view two specific breeds side-by-side to compare size, temperament, grooming needs, and more.
