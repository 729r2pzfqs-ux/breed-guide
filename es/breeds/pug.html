Descripción

Carlinos are charming, mischievous perros with expressive faces. They were bred to be compañeros and excel at job. They're known for their motto: 'multum in parvo' (much in little).

Temperamento

Carlino is known for being charming, mischievous, amoroso. They're excellent with niños and make wonderful family pets. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 13-15 años. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Low to moderate exercise necesita with short daily paseos and indoor play. They adapt well to vida en apartamento when their basic necesita are met. estimulación mental through interactivo toys keeps them engaged. gentil activities suit this raza's tranquilo temperament.
