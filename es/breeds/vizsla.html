Descripción

Vizslas are athletic Hungarian perro de cazas known as 'Velcro dogs' for their attachment to their owners. They're versatile hunters and amoroso compañeros.

Temperamento

The Vizsla is known for being cariñoso, enérgico, leal. They're excellent with niños and make wonderful family pets. Their intelligence and eagerness to please make entrenamiento enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 12-14 años. Common concerns include problemas articulares. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Alta energía breed requiring vigorous ejercicio diario—at least an hora of activity. They thrive with active familias who enjoy outdoor activities. estimulación mental through entrenamiento and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
