Descripción

A white variant developed from German Shepherds, el White Swiss Shepherd is gentil, inteligente, and eager to please. They're wonderful perro familiars.

Temperamento

The White Swiss Shepherd is known for being gentil, inteligente, leal. They're excellent with niños and make wonderful family pets. Their intelligence and eagerness to please make entrenamiento enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 12-14 años. Common concerns include displasia de cadera and hinchazón. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Alta energía raza requiring vigorous ejercicio diario—at least an hora of activity. They thrive with activo familias who enjoy outdoor activities. estimulación mental through entrenamiento and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
