Descripción

Named for their butterfly-like ears (papillon means butterfly in French), these tiny dogs are surprisingly athletic and inteligente. They excel in agility and obedience.

Temperamento

The Papillon is known for being alert, amigable, happy. Their intelligence and eagerness to please make entrenamiento enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 14-16 años. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Alta energía breed requiring vigorous ejercicio diario—at least an hora of activity. They thrive with active familias who enjoy outdoor activities. estimulación mental through entrenamiento and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
