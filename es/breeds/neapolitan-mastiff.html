Descripción

An ancient Italian guardian raza with dramatic loose skin and wrinkles. Mastín Napolitanos are devoted protectors who are tranquilo but always alert.

Temperamento

Mastín Napolitano is known for being leal, dignified, watchful. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 7-9 años. Common concerns include displasia de cadera and hinchazón. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Low to moderate exercise necesita with short daily paseos and indoor play. They adapt well to vida en apartamento when their basic necesita are met. estimulación mental through interactivo toys keeps them engaged. gentil activities suit this raza's tranquilo temperament.
