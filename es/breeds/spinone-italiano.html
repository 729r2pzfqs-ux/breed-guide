Descripción

An ancient Italian raza, el Spinone is one of most gentil and paciente pointing razas. Their wiry coat and soulful expression are distinctive.

Temperamento

The Spinone Italiano is known for being docile, amigable, paciente. They're excellent with niños and make wonderful family pets. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 10-12 años. Common concerns include displasia de cadera and hinchazón. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Moderate exercise necesita with daily paseos and regular play sessions. They enjoy a mix of physical activity and relaxation time. Interactivo games and entrenamiento sessions provide estimulación mental. A consistent exercise routine keeps them healthy and happy.
