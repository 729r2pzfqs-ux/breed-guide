Descripción

The 'Little Black Devil' es un small Belgian raza known for their curioso, mischievous nature. They're excellent perro guardiáns with a distinctive ruff and tailless appearance.

Temperamento

The Schipperke is known for being curioso, fearless, confident. They're excellent with niños and make wonderful family pets. Their intelligence and eagerness to please make entrenamiento enjoyable. Early socialization helps them develop into well-rounded compañeros.

Salud

Generalmente saludable with a lifespan of 12-14 años. Common concerns include patellar luxation and dental issues. Regular veterinary checkups and preventive care are important. Maintaining a healthy weight helps prevent many health issues.

Ejercicio

Alta energía raza requiring vigorous ejercicio diario—at least an hora of activity. They thrive with activo familias who enjoy outdoor activities. estimulación mental through entrenamiento and puzzle toys is equally important. Without adequate exercise, they may develop behavioral issues.
